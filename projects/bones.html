Bones

Website Fossil Repository
Web App
TL;DR: Bones is a small tool for managing remote Fossil-SCM repositories, giving an interface to find as well as create new Fossil repositories that can easily be shared and collaborated on remotely.

I’ve slowly been using Fossil for more of my personal projects. The idea of the self-contained nature of Fossil as well as the ease to host them remotely appeals to me a lot, but I wanted a nice interface for creating and navigating to the various repositories I’ve got. There’s already the great Chisel but I enjoy reinventing wheels, and was looking to build something which I could eventually integrate additional tooling into, such as a CI/CD pipeline and centralized auth.
